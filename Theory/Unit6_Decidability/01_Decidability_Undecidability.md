---

## 1.1 Decidable

**Regular Languages:**
- Membership, Emptiness, Equivalence: All decidable

**CFLs:**
- Membership, Emptiness: Decidable
- Equivalence, Ambiguity: Undecidable

---

## 1.2 Halting Problem

**Question:** Does TM M halt on w?

**Answer:** Undecidable

**Proof:** Diagonalization argument
